Rock Paper Scissors

To play this game:

  1. Right-click anywhere on the page and choose Inspect
  2. Or press the shortcut:
    • Mac: Command ⌘ + Option ⌥ + I
    • Windows: Ctrl + Shift + I or F12
  3. Click on the Console tab
  4. Follow the pop-up prompts to play 5 rounds
  5. The final result will be shown in both a pop-up and the console
